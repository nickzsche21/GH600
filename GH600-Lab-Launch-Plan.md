# GH600 Lab launch plan

## Brutal business model

Treat this as a **fast-cash certification wave**, not guaranteed recurring revenue.

The official GH-600 course does not become available until July 20, 2026. That creates a temporary preparation gap. The product must collect money while that gap is open, then expand into adjacent agent certifications only if customers prove the engine is valuable.

## Positioning

**Headline:** Pass GH-600 with hands-on agentic AI scenarios - not dumps.

**Promise:** Practice real-world agentic development scenarios across MCP servers, GitHub Actions, custom agents, YAML, JSON, logs, artifacts, guardrails and multi-agent workflows.

**Trust banner:** Original GH-600 practice lab. Public-doc based scenarios. No dumps. No memorization traps.

**Compliance line:** GH600 Lab is an independent, unofficial practice product and is not affiliated with GitHub, Microsoft, Pearson VUE, or the official GH-600 exam. All scenarios are original and based on public objectives and documentation. No recalled or unauthorized exam content is included.

The moat is practical artifact interpretation, not the number of multiple-choice questions.

## Launch pricing

- Free: 12-question diagnostic and six-domain readiness score.
- Founding Access: $29 lifetime for the first 100 buyers — 120 original scenarios across three
  full-length mock exams (MOCK_1–3) and a detailed readiness report. Upgradeable to Pro anytime.
- Pro: $49 — the full 300-scenario bank: all six mock exams (MOCK_1–6) plus 60 targeted
  weak-domain drills.
- Team Pack: $149 once for 10 seats on the Pro content set, manager readiness snapshot,
  domain-gap report and invoice.
- Urgent Cram Call: $99 for a limited 60-minute manual preparation session.

## Path to $5K launch revenue

The cleanest target is:

- 100 Founding Access buyers x $29 = $2,900
- 10 Team Packs x $149 = $1,490
- 7 Urgent Cram Calls x $99 = $693
- Total = $5,083

A more balanced target:

- 80 Founding Access buyers x $29 = $2,320
- 12 Team Packs x $149 = $1,788
- 10 Urgent Cram Calls x $99 = $990
- Total = $5,098 launch revenue

## Why this can work

1. The certification is new and the official training arrives on July 20.
2. The official exam may include interactive components.
3. Candidates report difficulty finding reliable, current preparation around MCP and multi-agent orchestration.
4. Search queries containing an exam code have strong buying intent.
5. Original YAML, JSON, log and configuration scenarios are harder to commoditize than generic flashcards.
6. Delivery cost is close to zero.

## First five days

### Day 1: Make payment possible

- Replace the local lead form with an email provider.
- Connect a payment option available to the business. Stripe India is invite-only for new accounts, so consider Razorpay, Lemon Squeezy, Paddle or manual UPI plus access codes for the first cohort.
- Keep delivery manual for the first 20 buyers if necessary.

### Day 2: Launch useful content

Post a practical breakdown such as "Five MCP and GitHub Actions mistakes GH-600 candidates should recognize." Link the free diagnostic after providing value.

Target communities carefully: r/GithubCopilot, r/AzureCertification, r/github and r/devops. Follow each community's self-promotion rules and disclose that you built the tool.

### Day 3-5: Pre-sell

The goal is 20 paid users before producing the full bank.

- 0-3 paid users: weak demand or weak distribution.
- 4-10 paid users: continue interviewing; distribution is likely the bottleneck.
- 10-25 paid users: real product; expand the artifact bank.
- 25+ paid users: focus on it for two weeks.

## Quality moat

- Every scenario gets an objective ID, source link, review status and last-verified date.
- Never use recalled, leaked or "real" exam questions.
- Two experienced reviewers should agree on each answer and explanation.
- Track which distractors learners choose and improve weak questions.
- Prioritize artifact labs: GitHub Actions YAML, MCP JSON, custom agent files, logs, traces and guardrail policies.

## Kill criteria

Stop or reposition after 300 qualified visitors if:

- Fewer than 20% start the diagnostic.
- Fewer than 50% of starters finish it.
- Fewer than 5% request paid access.
- Nobody prepays after 20 serious candidate conversations.

Do not build 180 questions before the market earns them.
